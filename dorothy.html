Dorothy

Dorothy Gale (nicknamed Dotty) is a fictional character created by L. Frank Baum, author and creator of the Oz Legacy. She is the orphan protagonist and adolescent heroine of Baum's first Oz book, The Wonderful Wizard of Oz, published in 1900 and which is considered one of the earliest American Fairytales written.
